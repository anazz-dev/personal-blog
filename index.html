Ahmad M. Nazzal

My name is Ahmad M. Nazzal, and I am a medical doctor and neuroscientist. I earned my doctorate in neuroscience from the International Max Planck Research School at the University of Göttingen, where I studied the neural correlates of decision-making in humans. My work on the brain sparked my interest in artificial intelligence (AI).

I have clinical experience in neurology, neuroradiology, and radiology. I helped several clinical AI startups succeed and I founded Clinical AI Academy—a platform dedicated to advancing AI literacy among healthcare professionals and helping clinical AI startups build tools that are both clinically relevant and compliant with emerging EU regulations.

Recently, I began working as a researcher at the ZAI Arabic Language Research Center, focusing on AI in education and the neuroscience of the Arabic language.
